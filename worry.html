🧘‍♀️ Exercise 🧘‍♂️

  • 🌬️ Breathe in through your nose for 4 seconds
  • 💨 Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  • 🌊 Slowly exhale through your mouth for 8 seconds
  • 🌱 Repeat for 3-5 cycles
  • 📿 While breathing, silently say: "I am safe. I am calm. I let go."
